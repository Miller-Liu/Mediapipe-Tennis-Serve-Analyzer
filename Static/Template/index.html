About this Project

During the 2021-2022 tennis season at my high school, I realized that I often found it difficult to compare my own swing with tennis pros like Nadal or Federer. To solve this issue, I used machine learning algorithms to produce a better way for tennis players compare their technique with professionals. While working on this project, I also realized that two separate machine learning approaches could both produce good results. The first approach uses clustering, while the second uses image recognition. Enjoy!
